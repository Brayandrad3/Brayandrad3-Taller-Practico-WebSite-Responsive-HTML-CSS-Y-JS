FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

ANSWERING YOUR MOST COMMON DOUBTS

What are the requirements to rent a car?

Generally, you need to be at least 21 years old (some models may require 25), a valid driver's license, and a credit card in your name for the security deposit.

What type of insurance do I need for the rental?

We offer several insurance options that you can choose at the time of booking. You can opt for our full coverage or use your credit card or personal insurance, if applicable.

Can I modify or cancel my reservation?

Yes, you can modify or cancel your reservation through our website or by contacting our support team. Cancellation fees may apply depending on the cancellation policy.

Is there a mileage limit?

Most of our rentals include unlimited mileage, but some specialized vehicles or promotional offers may have a limit. Please check your booking details.

What do I do if I have a problem with the car during the rental?

In case of any problem, please contact our 24/7 assistance line. We will provide you with the necessary instructions and support.
